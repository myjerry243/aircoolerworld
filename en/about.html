About ZUNTAI

Your trusted partner in ventilation and cooling since 2004

Guangdong Zuntai Electrical Appliance Technology Co., Ltd.

Located in the beautiful Pearl River Delta — Shunde, Foshan, Guangdong Province — ZUNTAI is a comprehensive enterprise integrating R&D, production, sales, and service of ventilation and cooling equipment.

Founded in 2004, we have grown to become one of the leading manufacturers in China's ventilation and cooling industry. Our factory covers an area of 7,000 square meters, equipped with advanced production lines and testing facilities.

With a dedicated team of over 320 employees, including 30+ engineers and technical staff, we continuously innovate to provide energy-efficient and environmentally friendly cooling solutions for our global customers.

Our products have been exported to more than 80 countries and regions, including Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, South America, and Europe. We have established long-term partnerships with renowned brands and distributors worldwide.

Our company's 48-hour on-site installation service, after branch offices everywhere, to provide customers with fast and perfect service.
