HVLS Fan

Our High Volume Low Speed (HVLS) fans are designed to move large volumes of air at low rotational speeds, creating a gentle and comfortable breeze throughout large spaces. With diameters ranging from 1.9m to 7.3m, these fans are the ideal solution for warehouses, factories, gymnasiums, exhibition halls, and other large commercial and industrial spaces.

Each fan features 6 aerodynamically designed blades and operates with a power consumption of just 0.4KW at 220V, making them extremely energy-efficient compared to traditional high-speed fans or air conditioning systems.

Key features include:

  • Diameters from 1.9m to 7.3m for various space sizes
  • 6 aerodynamic blades for optimal air movement
  • Ultra-low power consumption: 0.4KW, 220V
  • Variable frequency drive for precise speed control
  • Ultra-quiet operation
  • Year-round use — summer cooling and winter destratification
