Moveable Cylinder Brushless Fan

Our moveable cylinder brushless fans are designed for efficient, portable ventilation in confined and semi-enclosed spaces. Featuring advanced variable frequency drive (VFD) technology and brushless DC motors, these fans deliver exceptional airflow with remarkably low noise levels.

The compact cylindrical design makes them easy to position in tight spaces such as tunnels, underground facilities, workshops, and construction sites. The variable speed control allows precise adjustment of airflow to match specific ventilation requirements.

Key features include:

  • Variable frequency drive for stepless speed control
  • Brushless DC motor for high efficiency and long life
  • Ultra-low noise operation
  • Portable and lightweight design
  • Energy-efficient — consumes up to 60% less power than traditional fans
