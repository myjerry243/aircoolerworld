Negative Pressure Fan

Our negative pressure fans are designed to create powerful exhaust ventilation by drawing air out of a space, creating a negative pressure that pulls fresh air in through open windows or intake vents. This is the most effective way to remove heat, smoke, dust, and stale air from industrial environments.

Available in fiberglass and galvanized steel construction, our negative pressure fans are built to withstand harsh industrial conditions including high temperatures, humidity, and corrosive environments. They are widely used in factories, warehouses, greenhouses, livestock farms, and commercial kitchens.

Key features include:

  • Fiberglass or galvanized steel housing options
  • High-efficiency exhaust with low energy consumption
  • Corrosion-resistant construction for long service life
  • Belt-driven or direct-drive configurations
  • Easy maintenance with accessible components
