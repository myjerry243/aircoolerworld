Evaporative Air Cooler

Our evaporative air coolers use the natural process of water evaporation to cool the air, providing an energy-efficient and environmentally friendly alternative to traditional air conditioning. The principle is simple: hot air passes through water-soaked cooling pads, the water evaporates and absorbs heat, and cool, fresh air is circulated into the space.

Compared to compressor-based air conditioning, our evaporative coolers consume up to 80% less electricity while providing effective cooling with a temperature reduction of 5–15°C depending on ambient conditions.

Key features include:

  • Low power consumption — energy saving up to 80%
  • Natural and fresh air — no CFCs or refrigerants
  • Temperature reduction of 5–15°C
  • Easy installation and maintenance
  • Suitable for large open spaces
