Ventilation Pipeline

Our ventilation pipeline systems are custom-engineered ductwork solutions designed to efficiently distribute air throughout industrial, commercial, and agricultural facilities. Whether used for fresh air supply from evaporative coolers or exhaust air removal through negative pressure fans, our duct systems ensure optimal airflow and minimal pressure loss.

We offer a complete range of duct components including straight sections, elbows, reducers, dampers, diffusers, and air grilles in various materials to suit different application requirements. Our team provides full design, fabrication, and installation support.

Key features include:

  • Custom designed to your specific layout requirements
  • Available in galvanized steel, stainless steel, and aluminum
  • Round and rectangular configurations
  • Complete accessory range — dampers, diffusers, grilles
  • Low air resistance for energy-efficient operation
